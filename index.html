Short Intro to Halo

Halo CE was the first game of the Halo series, released in November 15th, 2001. Becoming a massive hit, it resulted in creating a whole game series about the world behind it. Even creating spin-offs in the form of books, animated series, and a TV show with their own takes on the world of Halo.

Short Coverage of Fan Halo Media

Aside from official media releases, it also inspired fans who loved the series, to create their own content of the world using the established characters or their own in a possible this might have happened as it was never shown or just extra stories with the fans' own spins/takes on aspects of Halo.
